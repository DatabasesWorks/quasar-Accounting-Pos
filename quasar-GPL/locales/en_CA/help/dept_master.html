Department Master

Name

The name of the Department.

Number

The number of the Department.

Points

Co-operative organizations sometimes allocate profits back to members based on points. Set the points assigned to every dollar of purchases for this department. Default is one.

Inactive?

Toggle on or off. Toggle on to set the department to inactive. Toggle off to re-set the department to active.
