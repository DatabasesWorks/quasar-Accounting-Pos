Shift Close



The shift close screen is used to process a shift close. The closing of a shift links all the transaction together for reporting purposes.

Store

Select the store in which the shift will be closed.

Station Table

  • Station - The name of the work station being closed.
  • # Trans - The number of transactions created during the shift.
  • Amount - The net dollar amount of transactions created during the shift.

Refresh

Clicking on refresh will refresh the data on the screen.

Close Shift

Click on the close shift button to close a shift for the station that is high-lighted.
