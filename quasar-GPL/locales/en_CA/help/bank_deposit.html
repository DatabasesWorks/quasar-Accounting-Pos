Bank Deposit




To transfer funds from the safe to the bank create a bank deposit. Following is an explanation of the attributes in the bank deposit screen:

Safe Station

The station that acts as the safe.

Safe Store

The store where the safe is located.

Bank Account

The bank account into which the deposit is being made.

Tender

The tender being deposited.

In Safe

Quasar displays the amount of each tender that should be in the safe.

Amount

Enter the amount of each tender being deposited.

Tender Total

The total of all tenders being deposited.
