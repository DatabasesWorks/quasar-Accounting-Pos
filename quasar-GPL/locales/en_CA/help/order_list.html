Order List



The order list displays the complete list of purchase orders. New purchase orders may be created and existing purchase orders may be viewed and edited.

List Table

  • Number - The identification number of the purchase order.
  • Date - The date the purchase order was created.
  • Vendor - The vendor the purchase order was placed with.
  • Amount - The total amount of the purchase order including item totals and purchasing taxes.
  • Closed - The state of the purchase order. If open, the remaining item orders on the purchase order are calculated as part of the items total on order.

Show Closed

Toggle on or off. Toggle on to display both closed and open purchase orders. Toggle off to display only open purchase orders.

New

Select this function to create a new purchase order.

Edit

Select this function to edit the high-lighted purchase order.

Refresh

Clicking on refresh will refresh the data on the screen.

Print

Select this function to print the contents of the list to the printer.
