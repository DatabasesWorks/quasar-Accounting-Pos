Adjustment Reason Master



The adjustment reason master file is used to define the reasons that you will adjust your inventory. Sample reasons for taking markdowns or writedowns are spoilage, theft, competition, damaged and advertising.

Name

The name of the inventory adjustment reason.

Number

The number of the inventory adjustment reason.

Account

When inventory is decreased or increased the inventory value is either increased or decreased accordingly. Enter the expense ledger account for the inventory decrease or increase.

Inactive?

Toggle on or off. Toggle on to set the adjustment reason to inactive. Toggle off to re-set the adjustment reason to active.
