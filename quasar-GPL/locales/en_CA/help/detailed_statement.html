Detailed Statement



The detailed statement screen displays the detail of items charged and customer payments for a specified time frame. The detailed statment also shows the total of all taxes for the period.

Customer

Enter the customer to view.

Store

The store to view.

From

The beginning date to view.

To

The end date to view.
