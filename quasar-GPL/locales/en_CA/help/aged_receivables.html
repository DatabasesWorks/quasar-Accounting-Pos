Aged Receivables



Aged receivables may be viewed by all customers, a customer type, a customer group or by individual customer.

All Customers




Toggle on to view all customers, customers linked to a specific customer type or customers linked to a specific customer group.
  • Type - enter the customer type to view
  • Group - enter the customer group to view
  • Pat Group - enter the pat group to view

Quasar has a handy drill down feature that allows you to drill down and view more detailed data. Simply double click on a line in the display table and Quasar will display the next level of detail. You can drill down directly into the transaction.

One Customer




Toggle on to view just one customer. Enter the customer to view.

Store

Select the store to view. Selecting a store displays only aged receivables from the selected store. All other aged receivable information is filtered out.

Display Table

The aged receivables window breaks receivable transactions down into aged periods based on the current date.
  • Current (0-29 days old)
  • 30 Days (30-59 days old)
  • 60 Days (60-89 days old)
  • 90 Days (90-119 days old)
  • 120+ Days (120-149 days old)

The totals are listed at the bottom of the window along with the percentage that each total is of the total receivable balance.

Refresh

Clicking on refresh will refresh the data on the screen.

Print

Select this function to print the contents of the aged receivable screen to the printer.
