POS Transactions




Once a point of sale transaction has been filed it may be viewed from either the transaction journal window or from the inquiry window. To view a transaction, from either window, double click on the transaction. The pos transaction window will be displayed.

From this window you may view all point of sale transactions. Following is a description of the attributes:

Id No

The identification number assigned to the transaction by Quasar Point-of-Sale.

Type

The type of transaction.

  • Nosale - transactions created when the cash drawer is opened for no definable purpose
  • Payout - transactions created when an expense is paid from the point of sale
  • Sale -transactions created for the sale of goods or services
  • Return - transactions created when products are returned
  • Ringoff - transactions created when a till is rung off
  • ROA - transactions created when a customer makes a payment on their receivable account

Date

The date the transaction was created.

Time

The time that the transaction was created.

Till

The identification number assigned to the till upon which the transaction was created.

Store

The store number assigned to the store in which the transaction was created.

Cashier

The name of the cashier who created the transaction.

Customer

The name of the customer.

Complete

A check-mark is displayed if the transaction has been finalized.

Training

A check-mark is displayed if the transaction was created using training mode.

Name

The memo that identifies the transaction.

Items Folder



View the items folder for the details of the items purchased or returned.

  • Item Number - the identification number of the item
  • Description - the item description
  • Quantity - the quantity of items purchased or returned
  • Price - the total price of the item
  • Voided - a check-mark is displayed if the item was voided from the transaction

Taxes Folder



View the items folder for the details of the items purchased or returned.

  • Tax - the tax id
  • Name - the name or description of the tax
  • Taxable - the taxable amount of items where the tax is not included in the price of the item
  • Tax Amt - total tax charged on items where the tax is not included in the price of the item
  • Inc. Taxable - the taxable amount of items where the tax is included in the price of the item
  • Inc. Tax Amt - total tax charged on items where the tax is included in the price of the item

Tenders



The tenders folder displays all the tenders used to tender the transaction.

  • Tender - the name of the tender
  • Amount - the amount tendered
  • Voided? - a check-mark is displayed if the tender was voided from the transaction

Accounts



The accounts folder displays all the accounts affected by the transaction.

  • Account - the name of the ledger account
  • Debit - the amount of any debit to the account
  • Credit - the amount of any credit to the account

Payment

Click on the payment button to allocate a payment, return or invoice. You may allocate a payment or return to an invoice, or alternatively, allocate an invoice to a payment or return.
