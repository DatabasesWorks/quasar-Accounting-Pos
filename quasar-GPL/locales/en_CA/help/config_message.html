Messages



This screen is used to define small messages for printed customer invoices, purchase orders and vendor invoices.

Customer Invoice

Enter a small message that will appear on customer invoices.

Purchase Order

Enter a small message that will appear on purchase orders

Vendor Invoice

Enter a small message that will appear on the vendor invoices.
