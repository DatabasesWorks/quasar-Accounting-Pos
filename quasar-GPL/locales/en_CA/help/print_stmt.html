Print Statements



The print statements screen is used to print statements for all customers, a select group of customers or a single customer.

All Customers

Toggle on to print statements for all customers.

Group Match

Toggle on to print statements for a selected group of customers.

One Customer

Toggle on to print a statement for a single customer.
